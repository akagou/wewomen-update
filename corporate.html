For organisations

Invest in the women who keep your organisation running.

Your most experienced employees are navigating a stage of life that affects energy, focus, sleep and long-term health. Most organisations do nothing about it.

WeWomen brings evidence-based health education directly into your workplace. We help women understand what's changing, build sustainable habits, and show up at their strongest.

Three ways in

Choose the format that fits your organisation

1 Full immersion

1-Day Empower & Elevate Event

One planned day, maximum impact.

  • All four topics covered in a single day
  • Follows the Understand → Recognise → Reset → Apply → Sustain framework
  • Opens with a personal intention, closes with a forward commitment
  • Maximum impact, minimum scheduling disruption
2 Depth & behaviour change

4-Week Foundations Programme

Depth, connection & real behaviour change.

  • One 90-minute session per week across four weeks
  • Peer commitment & midweek WhatsApp check-ins
  • Behaviour change woven into every session
  • Optional 30-minute workout add-on per session
3 Flexible

Deep Dive Workshops

Flexible, standalone sessions.

  • 90-minute sessions on specific topics
  • Women self-select what they need
  • Topics include nutrition, pelvic health, strength training & more
  • Ideal as ongoing L&D touchpoints throughout the year
